Media

Images are hosted on ImgBB. Upload here and they appear on the site immediately — no redeploy. Anything without an image keeps showing its gradient placeholder, so a half-finished catalogue never looks broken.

Hero slides

The four full-width slides on the home page. Landscape, roughly 2400×1200. Dark images work best — white text and a dark overlay sit on top.

Category tiles

The "Shop by Category" grid on the home page. A category appears on the home page once it has an image — so uploading artwork for Seats adds a seventh tile.

Partner logos

Shown on the About page. A square PNG works best.

A note on ImgBB. It is a free image host with no uptime guarantee, and images can be removed. If one disappears, the page falls back to its placeholder rather than breaking — but the photo is gone. For a shop that is taking real money, Supabase Storage (which you already pay for) is the safer home. Swapping is a single file: api/_lib/storage.js.
